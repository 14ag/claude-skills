# Content Patterns to Humanize

These patterns cover structural and content-based AI-isms that LLMs use to pad text and fake depth. When rewriting, target the underlying meaning, discard the fluff, and introduce real personality.

The patterns below apply to both English and Chinese texts. Look for equivalent phrases in the target language.

## 1. Undue Emphasis on Significance, Legacy, and Broader Trends
**English words to watch:** stands/serves as, is a testament/reminder, a vital/significant/crucial/pivotal/key role/moment, underscores/highlights its importance/significance, reflects broader, symbolizing its ongoing/enduring/lasting, contributing to the, setting the stage for, marking/shaping the, represents/marks a shift, key turning point, evolving landscape, focal point, indelible mark, deeply rooted
**Chinese words to watch:** 作为/充当、标志着、见证了、是……的体现/证明/提醒、极其重要的/重要的/至关重要的/核心的/关键性的作用/时刻、凸显/强调/彰显了其重要性/意义、反映了更广泛的、象征着其持续的/永恒的/持久的、为……做出贡献、为……奠定基础、标志着/塑造着、代表/标志着一个转变、关键转折点、不断演变的格局、焦点、不可磨灭的印记、深深植根于

**Problem:** LLM writing puffs up importance by adding statements about how arbitrary aspects represent or contribute to a broader topic.

**Humanization Strategy:** State the bare facts without the grand historical framing. Let the reader decide the importance.

## 2. Undue Emphasis on Notability and Media Coverage
**English words to watch:** independent coverage, local/regional/national media outlets, written by a leading expert, active social media presence
**Chinese words to watch:** 独立报道、地方/区域/国家媒体、由知名专家撰写、活跃的社交媒体账号

**Problem:** LLMs hit readers over the head with claims of notability, often listing generic sources without specific context.

**Humanization Strategy:** Replace generic lists of media outlets with one or two specific, contextualized examples.

## 3. Superficial Analyses with -ing Endings (or similar Chinese structures)
**English words to watch:** highlighting/underscoring/emphasizing..., ensuring..., reflecting/symbolizing..., contributing to..., cultivating/fostering..., encompassing..., showcasing...
**Chinese words to watch:** 突出/强调/彰显……、确保……、反映/象征……、为……做出贡献、培养/促进……、涵盖……、展示……

**Problem:** AI chatbots tack present participle ("-ing") phrases or their equivalents onto sentences to add fake depth and philosophical weight.

**Humanization Strategy:** Remove the abstract "-ing" phrase and state the concrete consequence or factual detail. 

## 4. Promotional and Advertisement-like Language
**English words to watch:** boasts a, vibrant, rich (figurative), profound, enhancing its, showcasing, exemplifies, commitment to, natural beauty, nestled, in the heart of, groundbreaking (figurative), renowned, breathtaking, must-visit, stunning
**Chinese words to watch:** 拥有（夸张用法）、充满活力的、丰富的（比喻）、深刻的、增强其、展示、体现、致力于、自然之美、坐落于、位于……的中心、开创性的（比喻）、著名的、令人叹为观止的、必游之地、迷人的

**Problem:** LLMs have serious problems keeping a neutral tone, especially for "cultural heritage" topics, leaning towards exaggerated promotional language.

**Humanization Strategy:** Tone down the adjectives. Describe traits factually rather than emotionally.

## 5. Vague Attributions and Weasel Words
**English words to watch:** Industry reports, Observers have cited, Experts argue, Some critics argue, several sources/publications (when few cited)
**Chinese words to watch:** 行业报告显示、观察者指出、专家认为、一些批评者认为、多个来源/出版物

**Problem:** AI chatbots attribute opinions to vague authorities ("Experts believe") without specific sources.

**Humanization Strategy:** Find or ask the user for the specific source, or remove the attribution and state the claim directly if it's common knowledge.

## 6. Outline-like "Challenges and Future Prospects" Sections
**English words to watch:** Despite its... faces several challenges..., Despite these challenges, Challenges and Legacy, Future Outlook
**Chinese words to watch:** 尽管其……面临若干挑战……、尽管存在这些挑战、挑战与遗产、未来展望

**Problem:** Many LLM-generated articles include formulaic, balanced "Challenges" or "Future Outlook" sections to wrap up an essay symmetrically.

**Humanization Strategy:** If challenges must be mentioned, integrate them specifically into the narrative rather than isolating them in a generic concluding section.
